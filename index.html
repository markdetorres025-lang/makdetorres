About the Product

Click & Catch is an interactive campus reward booth that combines a digital token system with a physical claw machine containing capsule prizes. Students first visit the Click & Catch website to claim digital tokens. These tokens are then presented to booth staff, who manually verify them before activating the claw machine.

Each capsule contains small, practical, student-friendly prizes such as campus sticker packs, USB port plugs, crochet cable ties, mini stationery items, or a special capsule labeled “Custom Phone Charm.”

Target Market: Students, Gamers.

Unique Selling Proposition: We help college students by providing an interactive claw machine experience where they can win student-friendly prizes and personalized custom phone charms, combining entertainment, creativity, and instant satisfaction in a campus-friendly booth.
